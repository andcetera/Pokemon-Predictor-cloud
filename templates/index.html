Tiering Overview

Smogon is a competititve Pokémon battling community that organizes Pokémon by tiers based on their usage. Pokémon of a certain tier are banned from lower tiers, to allow Pokémon in those tiers to be able to battle against Pokémon of similar power. Occasionally, Pokémon have to be banned from a tier by the community due to being game-breaking in one tier but not being used often enough in the tier above. There are also additional tier designations for Pokémon that only see use in certain formats.

Tiers:

  1. OU (OverUsed)
  2. UU (UnderUsed)
  3. RU (RarelyUsed)
  4. NU (NeverUsed)
  5. PU (Because they stink)

Banlists:

  1. Uber (OUBL)
  2. UUBL
  3. RUBL
  4. NUBL
  5. PUBL

Other Tiers:

  • AG (Anything Goes) – Every rule except "don't do anything that makes the battle last indefinitely" is lifted. One Pokémon, Mega Rayquaza, can only be used in this format.
  • NFE (Not Fully Evolved) – Pokémon that can still evolve
  • LC (Little Cup) – Pokémon that have never evolved, but can
  • Untiered – Pokémon who players are strongly advised not to use, due to low power
